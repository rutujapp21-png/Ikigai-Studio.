About the Studio

A studio built
on intent.

"Meaningful design is not just created — it is experienced, remembered, and built to scale."
Rutuja Pawar
Founder & Creative Strategist

Ikigai Studio is a purpose-driven creative and digital studio shaping brands that are not only seen — but deeply felt. Founded by Rutuja Pawar, the studio brings together strategy, aesthetics, and storytelling to craft identities with clarity and intent.

Rutuja's exposure to London's design and hospitality landscape brings a global lens to every project — blending international sophistication with cultural intelligence. This refined perspective shapes work that connects universally while remaining deeply intentional.

Specialising in hospitality and lifestyle brands, Ikigai Studio creates experiences that go beyond visuals. From brand identity to content and performance marketing — including Meta ads — every touchpoint is designed to connect emotionally and deliver measurable impact.

At its core, Ikigai Studio believes that meaningful design is not just created. It is experienced, remembered, and built to scale.
